OUSA Art Week Installation

Interactive installation based on my final year university project looking at gender stereotypes, but placed in a more public space in order to gauge a response and help develop the project further. The public are invited to create their own characters/stories with the magnetic pieces, or draw on the whiteboard or paper templates. "Imagine if" prompt cards are provided and viewers are encouraged to write their own to help inspire others.

Illustrator, magnetic whiteboard, magnetic dress-up pieces
