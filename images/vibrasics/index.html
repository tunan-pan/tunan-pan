An update! (Check us at myspace)

Much has happened since the last update over a year ago, Ian (Des MC) has left after deciding he could no longer bear the injustice of Dunedin's instant noodle prices and departed for the MSG coated shores of Hong Kong. While Aidan's (The odd keyboard guy) sweet tooth and need for legal medical marijuana have forced him to take up residency in Canada. Mikey (turntables) likes to munch on whales and it wasn't going down well here, so he has joined a whale munching club in Japan.

Our new members: Paul Young (saxes), Paul Raine (keys) Oh, and Matt is back on bass.

Anyways, until this site is revamped, for a more frequently updated account of our exploits + tour/gig dates go to our myspace page: www.myspace.com/vibrasics
