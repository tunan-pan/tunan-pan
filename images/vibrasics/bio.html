Vibrasics is a live, 7 piece Hip Hop band based in Dunedin.

Comprising of

Stu Harwooddrums
Matt Brookbass
Aidan Fraserkeys
Ian McKinlayvocals
Dave Goodsaxes + vocals
Lewis Waitetrumpet
Michael Aslinturntables

The group has been playing together since 2002. These musicians have also appeared in many other touring Dunedin acts.

The Vibrasics style of Hip Hop owes as much to classic funk, soul and jazz as it does to the proponents of modern hip hop. In true hip hop fashion, our set takes a wide range of musical elements and fashions them into something new, encompassing reggae, dancehall, drum n' bass, electro and whatever else takes our fancy. Audiences cannot resist getting up off their seats.

Recent highlights for the band include opening for The Black Seeds and Dimmer at University Orientation in February and playing at Auckland's Funk Super-bowl.

The future for Vibrasics is looking hot: We have recorded our debut LP in the beautiful environment of the Catlins, set to be released late November on Borderline Recordings.
