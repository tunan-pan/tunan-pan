donations

We've just moved into a purpose built cat shelter in Kingston, replacing the one in Brooklyn. As you can imagine, this was a costly exercise for us, so any help you can give would be greatly appreciated!

You can post your donation to The Cats Protection League Wgtn, PO Box 12157, Thorndon, Wellington.

Items we need:

  1. Good quality cat food (wet and dry)
  2. blankets, towels, and other bedding materials
  3. newspapers
  4. kitty litter

Alternatively, click the Donate button below to make an online donation to the Cats Protection League using PayPal.

How to donate:

  1. Click on the donate button above to go to the PayPal donation site.
  2. Enter the amount you wish to donate and click the Update Total button.
  3. If you wish to donate by credit card, look for the text
    "Don't have a PayPal account? Use your credit card or bank account (where available). Continue"
    and click the Continue link. If you have a PayPal account you can donate to us using that, and probably don't need any more help.
  4. Otherwise, enter your credit card payment details and click the "Review donation and continue" button. Note: The phone number format they require is areacode phonenumber eg 04xxxxxxx (do not include country code).
  5. Check your details on the donation confirmation screen and if correct, click the "Donate $xx NZD" now button.
  6. PayPal will email you a donation receipt.

IMPORTANT: If you wish to claim a tax rebate on your donation:
Please click the "print your donation receipt" on the PayPal donation screen and print the resulting page. The NZ Inland Revenue will NOT accept the emailed receipt as proof of donation as it lacks the date of donation in the body of the email. The printed reciept however contains all the information required by the Inland Revenue to claim tax back on your donation.
