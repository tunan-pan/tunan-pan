Contact us

To contact us you can call us on 0064 4 3899 668 and leave a message. Someone will get back to you as soon as possible.

Alternatively send an email to catsprotectionleaguewgtn@hotmail.com

For donations you can post to us at:
The Cats Protection League Wellington
PO Box 12157
Thorndon
Wellington

The shelter is open to the public every Saturday and Sunday from 12-4pm. No appointment is required. Cash or cheque (with card) only please. We do not have EFTPOS available.

If you would like to join the CPL or offer your support, download a membership subscription form or register online.
