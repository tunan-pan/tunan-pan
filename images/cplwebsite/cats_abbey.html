Abbey
  1. Sex: Female
  2. Age: 8 years
  3. Colour: Tabby and white
  4. Hair: Short
  5. Status: Has been adopted
  6. Shelter: Kingston

Abbey has already been adopted into a loving home. If you are the adoptee, you can sign in now to post updates and photos about Abbey's new family and life!

Abbey, her brother Simba and sister Amber came to us when their owner moved to Australia and was unable to take them.

Abbey is very affectionate and will sometimes sulk if she thinks she's not getting enough attention. Sometimes she'll cry if she thinks she's been left on her own and will come running for love when called.

All three of them love to sleep on the bed and will sometimes climb under the duvet if it's cold. They're used to getting a lot of attention, are great with children and love being spoken to.

Ideally we'd like to home all three together, but realise that this may not be possible. Their person feels that Abbey would be the fastest to adapt if separated from the other two so we would look at homing her individually to the right home. One where there was someone home for most of the day would suit her best.
