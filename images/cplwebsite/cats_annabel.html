Annabel
  1. Sex: Female
  2. Age: 11-12 years
  3. Colour: White and tabby
  4. Hair: Short
  5. Status: Not available for Adoption
  6. Shelter: Kingston

An ex-feral cat, Annabel came to us in 2001 and has never really come around to thinking humans are OK.

She has found a good friend in little Caruso and the two of them can often be seen curled up in a basket together.

Unfortunately, Annabel is not available for adoption, but you can help by sponsoring her today to make sure she still gets the care she needs.
