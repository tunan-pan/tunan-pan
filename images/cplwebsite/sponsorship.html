sponsorship programme

One of the most rewarding ways you can help CPL is through our Sponsorship Programme.

Some of the cats we care for are not adoptable because they are on long term medication, are elderly, or have problems arising from their lives before they came to us and for a few, now call the shelter home.

For a regular monthly donation, starting at $10.00 per month, sponsors will receive:

  1. a certificate of sponsorship with a photograph of your chosen sponsored cat
  2. regular updates on the health and well-being of your sponsored cat
  3. that warm, fuzzy feeling you get when you are doing something for an animal in need!
  4. an open invitation to visit your sponsored cat at our Kingston shelter.

If you'd like to sponsor one of our cats, please download our sponsorship form, or contact us to arrange for a sponsorship pack to be mailed to you.
