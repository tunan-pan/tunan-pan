News

14 September 2009 by CPL

Want to help but can't adopt? You could sponsor one of our "special needs" cats.

Some of the cats we care for are not adoptable because they are on long term medication, are elderly, or have problems arising from their lives before they came to us and because a few now call the shelter home.

For a regular monthly donation, starting at $10.00 per month, you could help the CPL continue to give these cats the care they so richly deserve.

Click here to find out more about our Sponsorship Programme and to see the cats available for sponsorship.

06 July 2006 by CPL

Volunteers urgently needed

The Wellington Cats Protection League runs a shelter in Kingston, Wellington where we care for and find new homes for the cats and kittens who come into our care.

As a not-for-profit charity, we rely on our volunteers to cover the morning and evening shifts, 7 days a week that it takes to keep our cats fed and the shelter cleaned.

Some volunteers come on a weekly basis, some fortnightly and the time that they give to the cats is greatly appreciated by the League.

The morning shifts involve feeding the cats, cleaning their cages and litter boxes and generally tidying up the shelter. And of course, there are large numbers of cats who need cuddling and loving while you're at it!

Afternoon shifts involve scooping out litter boxes, feeding and of course lots of cat patting.

If you would like to help us care for our wonderful cats until we're able to find new homes for them, please call 04 3899668, leave a message and one of us will get back to you, or email us on catsprotectionleaguewgtn@hotmail.com
