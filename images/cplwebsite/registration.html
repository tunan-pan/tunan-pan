join the team

By subscribing as a member you are helping support us through an annual subscription. There are several hundred financial members; new members are always welcome.

The annual subscription is modest and includes a copy of our newsletter, published quarterly. Life membership and Junior membership are also available.

Or you can register to join our online team and update news or information on already adopted cats, as well as communicate with other registered members.

You do not have to subscribe to join our online community, nor are you obligated to volunteer.
